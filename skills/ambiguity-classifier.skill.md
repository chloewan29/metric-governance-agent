# Ambiguity classifier

## Classify each supported issue

- **Same label, different meaning:** identical wording points to different concepts.
- **Different labels, same meaning:** aliases may refer to one concept; require confirmation before merging.
- **Formula conflict:** calculations, joins, inclusions, or exclusions differ.
- **Time basis conflict:** event dates, close dates, recognition dates, periods, or timezones differ.
- **Grain conflict:** customer, account, opportunity, transaction, or reporting grains differ.
- **Filter conflict:** population, status, region, product, or eligibility filters differ.
- **Owner gap:** no accountable owner is evidenced.
- **Executive usage risk:** an ambiguous or incomplete metric is used in board, investor, regulatory, or executive reporting.

## Method

1. Cite evidence IDs and source files for every issue.
2. Explain the business risk without asserting which variant is correct.
3. Keep legitimate variants separate.
4. Write a specific owner question or next decision for each issue.
5. Mark unsupported or low-confidence interpretations for review.
