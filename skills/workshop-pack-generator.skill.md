# Workshop pack generator

## Build the pack

1. Summarize the decision context and evidence without choosing a preferred definition.
2. Group questions by metric variant and accountable stakeholder.
3. Ask for each candidate metric:
   - definition, owner, source of truth, and formula
   - grain and time basis
   - approved and not-approved uses
   - caveats, related metrics, and review cadence
   - naming, rename, split, deprecate, or investigate decision
4. Offer explicit decision options: Certify, Rename, Split, Deprecate, or Investigate.
5. Include evidence references, open questions, decision owners, and follow-up dates.

## Guardrails

- Do not frame the agent's inference as stakeholder agreement.
- Do not force one definition when multiple variants serve legitimate uses.
- Leave unanswered questions visible in the workshop record.
