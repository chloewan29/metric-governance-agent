# Revenue alignment workshop notes

Finance confirmed the Board Pack should use Recognised Revenue from the monthly finance close, net of credits and excluding GST.
Sales Ops confirmed the Salesforce dashboard is not finance revenue. It is closed-won opportunity value and should be renamed Booked Revenue.
Marketing agreed that Pipeline Revenue should be renamed Pipeline Value because it represents open and qualified opportunity value, not realised revenue.
Analytics will update the catalog and create a dashboard label change plan.
